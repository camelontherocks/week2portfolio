Bob IS the camel on the rocks. He's tall, thin, not much hair, large nose, and lives on a rocky outcroping.

Bob's Boat

Bob has a Sea Sport Explorer named "Cormorant". It's a small, but capable, boat built for the Pacific N.W. with a radar, autopilot, and inboard 260 hp diesel engine.

The Cormorant can sleep two, and has a refrigerator, stove, sink, dinette, and of course a head (toilet). We can live on this boat for days!!!

About The SeaSport Explorer

  • Local - Made in Bellingham, right here in the Pacific N.W..
  • Capable - Long range with two 60 gallon tanks.
  • Solid - Very solid/heavy construction.
  • Versatile - You can fish, crab, shrimp, or just cruise.
